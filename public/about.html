Our Mission

We believe no lost item should stay lost forever. Our platform connects students to help return misplaced belongings quickly and safely across the campus.

How It Works

🔍

1. Lost or Find

Realize you've lost something, or find an item that belongs to someone else on campus.

📝

2. Report It

Quickly fill out a short report with the item's details, location, and your contact info.

🤝

3. Reconnect

Browse the items list, contact the finder or loser, and safely return the item!
